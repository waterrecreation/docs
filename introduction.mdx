## About InFancy

The first step to world-class documentation is setting up your editing environments.

The introduction of computers, driven by semiconductor technology, has dramatically reduced the cost of complex **calculations**, enabling humanity to surmount numerous technological hurdles. The advent of the Internet has facilitated the near-zero margin cost of **information** distribution, vastly improving the efficiency of information creation and dissemination.

Today, **Artificial Intelligence**, specifically Large Language Model-based, has evolved to offer the ability to **create** and reason, enabling it to interact with human-like abilities. Unlike previous productivity tools, Generative AI can turn human imagination into a digital format for display or execution. 

This has especially benefited the content creation industry, significantly reducing the marginal cost of content and knowledge production. This emerging **productivity revolution** will ultimately lead to a paradigm shift in human life and work, and also reshape our social fabric and cultural norms.

Generative AI is still in its infancy, reminiscent of our ancestors' initial discovery of fire or the unlocking of language. There is still significant potential in Generative AI that has not yet been fully realized. It's time to unleash it for everyone.

Thus, we introduce Infancy.ai, a community dedicated to sowing and cultivating the seeds of knowledge in Generative AI, fostering growth and exploration in this burgeoning field.

## **Our Vision:**

Where everyone can decode their imagination into a fancy world.

## **Our Mission**

- **Democratize Knowledge**: We believe in the power of knowledge and aim to democratize the fundamentals and latest advancements of Generative AI through online seminars, workshops, and lectures.
- **Lower Barriers**: We will provide easy-to-understand tutorials, case studies, and practical guides to help beginners quickly get started, making AI a powerful tool in their hands.
- **Foster Communication**: We encourage interaction and sharing among community members by establishing a vibrant exchange environment through forums, blogs, and social media.
- **Inspire Innovation**: We support and encourage innovative thinking, offering resources and support to individuals and teams daring to experiment and brave in practice.
- **Enhance Efficiency**: We believe that by mastering and applying Generative AI, everyone can achieve a leap in productivity and creativity in their work.

## **Our Values**

- **Openness**: We welcome talents from different backgrounds and with various skills to join us and collectively promote the development of the community.
- **Mutual Assistance and Cooperation**: We advocate for mutual help and cooperation among members, sharing knowledge and experience to grow together.
- **Continuous Learning**: We encourage continuous learning and self-improvement, keeping pace with technological development and constantly exploring unknown territories.
- **Respect for Diversity**: We respect the opinions and creativity of every member, believing diversity is the source of innovation and progress.
- **Responsibility**: We recognize that the use of technology requires a responsible attitude and advocate for the reasonable and ethical use of Generative AI.

## Call to Action

Whether you are an AI expert, a tech enthusiast, or a newbie curious about artificial intelligence, the [Infancy.ai](http://infancy.ai/) community welcomes you. Let's explore the infinite possibilities of Generative AI together and co-create a more intelligent, efficient future.
